Features

Everything a lean team needs, nothing it doesn't.

Six tools that cover planning, focus, and visibility — without the setup tax of heavier project software.

Kanban boards with WIP limits

Drag work through columns you define, and cap how much can sit "in progress" so bottlenecks show up before they cost you.

Built‑in Pomodoro timers

Attach focus sessions right to a task so time tracking happens as work gets done, not in a separate spreadsheet after.

Flow & forecast analytics

Cumulative flow, burndown, throughput and cycle‑time charts, plus Monte Carlo forecasts to predict realistic delivery dates.

Swimlanes & custom fields

Split boards by team, product or priority, and capture the details unique to your workflow with fields you define.

Real‑time collaboration

Everyone sees moves, comments and edits the instant they happen — no refresh, no "who has the latest version".

Mobile web app

Add it to your home screen and check in from anywhere — swipe between columns, drag cards, and catch up on comments.

Analytics

See bottlenecks before they cost you.

enkl turns every move on the board into data — so you're reacting to what's actually happening, not what a status meeting remembers.

  • CFDCumulative flow diagrams that show exactly where work queues up.
  • BRNBurndown charts tracking remaining work against your timeline.
  • THRThroughput & cycle‑time reports for realistic planning.
  • MCSMonte Carlo forecasting to predict delivery dates with a confidence range.
